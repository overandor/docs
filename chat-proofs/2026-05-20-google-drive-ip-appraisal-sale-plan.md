## Key risk finding

The session identified a serious security issue: at least one archived code/chat artifact appeared to contain hardcoded credentials. The exact secrets are intentionally not reproduced here. The corrective action is mandatory before any sale, bank review, grant submission, or public listing:

- rotate exposed keys;
- delete raw secrets and token files;
- scrub old chat exports that quote credentials;
- replace credentials with `.env.example` placeholders;
- document cleanup in `SECURITY_CLEANUP.md`.
